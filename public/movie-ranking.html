The Best Movies According to Leslie

My top 8 movies of all-time.

The Conjuring 2

Really catchy and entertaining!

Insidious

Good plot, I was glued until the end. Also, Very good sequel.

Deadsilence

Very well-made, amazing plot and environment.

High School Musical

My childhood favorite!

Hamilton

Recent discovery, very insightful and educative.

In the Heights

Makes any Latino person feel represented!

Before Sunrise

Very insightful, did not expect this level of openness in a 1995 movie.

Knives Out

Makes me feel represented as an immigrant, also good choice for a movie night with friends!
